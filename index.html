Biography

Patric Lee's Chinese name is Li Pengcheng. In Chinese, Peng, transforming from a fish called Kun which lived in the North Sea, is an imaginary bird with strong wings. "Pengcheng" means literally that the powerful bird begins a flight to the sky, in the meanwhile as a metaphor that someone ambitious is ready to achieve his or her goal.

In 2014, Patric got a fairly high score in Mainland China's College Entrance Examination and chose to study in Peking University. After hesitating for one year, he finally decided to major in computer science, which, he believed, is an intellectually insteresting, financially promising and technically practically subject. Especially Patric wishes to do some researches on natural language processing and machine learning.

In August 2016, he headed for Hong Kong University of Science and Technology as an exchange student for one semester.
